About AURONET

Welcome to Auronet Lanka — your trusted partner in network infrastructure, CCTV installation, access control systems, and structured cabling solutions. Based in 553/2, Pitipana South, Homagama, we are committed to delivering high-quality, professional services that secure and connect your spaces with confidence.


Our Story

Founded in 2010, Auronet began as a tech repair service and has since evolved into a comprehensive solutions provider in electronic security and IT infrastructure. Driven by a passion for technology and a strong foundation in hands-on technical work, we’ve grown to serve homes, offices, and industries across Sri Lanka with modern, reliable, and scalable infrastructure systems. Our mission is to offer professional-grade solutions that are affordable, secure, and designed to last.


We Provide Best Service

At Auronet, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service in every project we undertake. Our commitment to quality ensures that your security and networking systems receive expert attention and care. With a team of skilled technicians using the latest tools and techniques, we provide reliable solutions across a range of services including CCTV installation, access control systems, computer networking, and infrastructure cabling. Whether it's a small-scale setup or a complex installation, we are dedicated to exceeding your expectations with efficient, top-tier service.

We specialize in the following services:

  • CCTV Installation – IP, analog & hybrid systems with remote viewing
  • SAccess Control Systems – Biometric, RFID & keypad door entry
  • Structured Cabling – Organized, high-performance cabling for voice/data
  • Network Infrastructure Design – Planning and building reliable networks
  • Fiber Optic Installation – High-speed connectivity for modern businesses
  • Preventive Maintenance – Regular servicing to avoid unexpected failures
  • Consultation & Support – Helping you choose the right solutions
